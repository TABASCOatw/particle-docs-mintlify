# Mapping network coverage

Particle's Wallet Abstraction solutions support a significant amount of EVM networks (roughly 80) alongside Solana and Tron, although to varying degrees. The full list with details on chain-specific support across the Particle Network tech stack can be found below.

<Warning>
  <p>
    <strong>
      Not all of the below are supported by Particle's Smart
      Wallet-as-a-Service.
    </strong>
  </p>
  <p>
    Many of these chains only have EOA support, which means that account
    abstraction through Particle's Smart Wallet-as-a-Service isn't possible, and
    thus are exclusively available within the traditional EOA-based
    Wallet-as-a-Service.
  </p>
</Warning>

---
